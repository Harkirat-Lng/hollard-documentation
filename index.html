About Hollard Money

Hollard Money is a comprehensive digital platform designed to help everyday South Africans understand, monitor, and improve their credit health. In partnership with leading financial institutions, we provide consumers with free access to their credit information, actionable insights, and a pathway to better financial wellbeing.

The Challenge We Address

Many South Africans struggle with understanding their credit profile and how it impacts their financial opportunities. Without easy access to credit information and guidance, consumers often make uninformed financial decisions, leading to over-indebtedness, declined applications, and missed opportunities for better financial products.

Our Solution

Hollard Money bridges this gap by providing a free, easy-to-use platform where consumers can view their credit score, understand the factors affecting it, receive personalised coaching, and connect with suitable financial products—all supported by dedicated credit coaches who guide users on their financial journey.
